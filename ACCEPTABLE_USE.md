## What this app is

"The Bard's Song" (the "App") is a **general-purpose creative
tool**. You provide the inputs — photos, names, descriptions, prompts — and AI
models turn them into comic pages.

The App:

- **bundles no third-party intellectual property** (no game publisher's
  artwork, logos, characters, factions, settings, or named content);
- is **not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by** any game publisher,
  including (without limitation) Wizards of the Coast / Hasbro (Dungeons &
  Dragons) or Games Workshop (Warhammer / Warhammer 40,000);
- treats generic genre aesthetics (e.g. "high fantasy", "grimdark sci-fi") as
  free-to-use tropes — it does **not** provide or encourage reproduction of any
  specific protected brand, character, or property.


## Your responsibilities

You are solely responsible for the content you upload, the prompts you write,
and how you use anything the App generates. By using the App you confirm that:

1. **Rights to inputs** — you own or have permission to use every photo, image,
   name, and description you provide.
2. **Likeness & consent** — you will not upload photos of real people without
   their consent. For **minors**, you must be a parent/guardian or have explicit
   guardian consent. (Safe Mode is designed for, and recommended when, children
   use or appear in the App.)
3. **No third-party IP infringement** — you will not use the App to reproduce or
   distribute content that infringes someone else's copyright or trademark
   (e.g. branded characters, factions, or settings from tabletop or video
   games). Making a comic of *your own* miniatures/characters for your *own*
   group is your responsibility to keep within the rights you hold.
4. **Compliance** — you will comply with the terms of the AI providers you
   configure (see below) and with applicable law where you live.

## Prohibited uses

Do not use the App to create, store, or share content that:

- sexualizes or endangers minors in any way;
- is hateful, harassing, or promotes violence against people based on protected
  characteristics;
- is sexually explicit, gratuitously violent, or otherwise unlawful;
- impersonates a real person to deceive, or depicts a real person without
  consent;
- infringes the intellectual property or privacy rights of others.

The App includes a **Safe Mode** (on by default) that moderates inputs, adds
content guardrails to prompts, and sanitizes output. Safe Mode is a heuristic
aid, **not a guarantee** — adult supervision is advised for young creators.


## Third-party AI providers

Story and art are generated by external services you configure (e.g. Google
Gemini, OpenAI-compatible endpoints, ElevenLabs, or a local model). Your use of
those services — and the ownership and permitted use of their outputs — is
governed by **their** terms and policies. You are responsible for holding valid
access and for using outputs within those terms. The App ships no API keys.

## Generated content

AI-generated output may be imperfect, may unintentionally resemble existing
works, and may have limited or no copyright protection depending on your
jurisdiction. You use generated content at your own risk.
